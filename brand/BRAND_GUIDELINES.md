| Role | Font | Style | Size (stories) |
|------|------|-------|---------------|
| Display / captions | Cormorant Garamond | Italic | 64–84px |
| Logo / UI | DM Sans | Light (300) | 24–32px |
| Body text (if used) | DM Sans | Regular | 28–36px |

## Logo
The De Luca logo is a silver/grey horizontal badge with "De Luca" in DM Sans Light, crimson coloured text.

**Placement:**
- Stories: bottom-centre, ~100px from bottom edge
- Feed posts: bottom-left or bottom-centre
- Never: top-right (reserved for Instagram UI)

## Voice & Tone
**Think:** A charming Italian host, not a marketing department.

### Do:
- Italian-inflected words and phrases: Bellissimo, Salute, Cucina, Al fresco
- Rhetorical questions that tease: "Fancy a bite?" / "The view? Bellissimo."
- Short, confident lines — let the photography lead
- Reference Cambridge warmly ("the city's best rooftop view")
- Celebrate occasions: graduation, date nights, groups, birthdays

### Don't:
- Discount/sale language: "CHEAP", "DEAL", "LIMITED OFFER"
- Emoji overload — one max, or none
- Generic food hashtags: #foodie #yummy #food #instafood
- Corporate speak or stiff formal tone
- Long captions over 200 characters on feed posts
- ALL CAPS (except stylistic sparingly)

## Content Pillars
1. **Food** — pasta, pizza, risotto, gnocchi, linguine frutti di mare, gelato
2. **Terrace** — rooftop views, al fresco dining, Cambridge skyline
3. **Piano Bar** — Fri/Sat nights, cocktails, live entertainment, atmosphere
4. **Occasions** — graduation, date night, groups, private dining
5. **Behind the scenes** — open kitchen, chef craft, local ingredients
