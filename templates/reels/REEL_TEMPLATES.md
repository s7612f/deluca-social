## REEL 1: Dish Reveal
**Duration:** 8–12 seconds
**Best for:** Food posts, new menu items, weekly specials

### Shot list:
1. Black/dark frame (0.5s) — logo or restaurant name fade in
2. Wide shot of dish arriving at table or being plated (2s)
3. Close-up: fork touching, steam rising, sauce glistening (3s)
4. Pull back to show full plate + table setting (2s)
5. Title card: dish name in Cormorant Garamond Italic on crimson bg (2s)
6. De Luca logo fade out (1s)

### CapCut setup:
- Text style: Cormorant Garamond (or similar serif italic), white, 60–70sp
- Transitions: hard cut or 0.3s cross-dissolve only
- Colour grade: increase warmth +15, contrast +10, slightly darken shadows
- Audio: trending food/ambient track OR "no audio" for story posts

### Caption for this reel:
Handmade. Every single time.
#DeLucaCambridge #CambridgeEats #ItalianFood #HandmadePasta

## REEL 3: Piano Bar Night
**Duration:** 15–30 seconds
**Best for:** Thursday/Friday posts, weekend push

### Shot list:
1. Exterior / staircase going up to Piano Bar (2s)
2. Candlelit cocktail glass close-up (2s)
3. Piano keys / hands playing (3s)
4. Crowd at bar, people smiling and singing (3s)
5. Singer/pianist wide shot (3s)
6. Title card: "Every Friday & Saturday" — crimson bg, white serif text (3s)
7. "From 8pm · The Piano Bar at De Luca" — DM Sans, smaller (2s)
8. Logo (1s)

### CapCut setup:
- Colour grade: moody/warm, reduce highlights, rich blacks
- Transitions: hard cuts — no fades (more energy)
- Audio: 5–10 second clip of live performance OR upbeat jazz track
- Text: minimal — only the title card and timing

### Caption:
Cambridge's favourite Friday night. Cocktails. Requests. Sing-alongs. From 8pm.
#PianoBar #CambridgeNightOut #CambridgeBar #DeLucaCambridge #LiveMusic

## REEL 4: Behind the Scenes
**Duration:** 20–30 seconds
**Best for:** Midweek engagement, brand storytelling

### Shot list:
1. Market/fresh ingredients arriving (2s)
2. Chef hands: chopping, seasoning, stirring (3s)
3. Pasta being rolled or shaped (3s)
4. Pizza being stretched and topped (3s)
5. Plating close-up (3s)
6. Dish leaving kitchen to table (2s)
7. Guests reacting/enjoying (2s)
8. Title card: "Made from scratch. Every day." (3s)
9. Logo fade (1s)

### CapCut setup:
- Handheld feel — don't stabilise too much
- Use CapCut "Auto Captions" to add subtitles if any talking-to-camera
- Audio: kitchen ambience sound + upbeat background track layered
- Transitions: hard cut — keep energetic

### Caption:
From scratch. From the heart. Come taste the difference.
#DeLucaCambridge #BehindTheScenes #ItalianFood #CambridgeEats #HandmadeFood

## Final Cut Pro — Project Setup

### New project settings:
- Name: De Luca [Month] [Year]
- Resolution: 1080 × 1920
- Frame rate: 30fps
- Audio: Stereo, 48kHz

### Saved elements to build once:

**Lower thirds title:**
- Background: #6E0B1C rectangle, ~900px wide, 120px tall, bottom 30% of frame
- Text: Cormorant Garamond Italic, white, 64pt
- Subtitle: DM Sans Light, white 70% opacity, 28pt
- Save as a Motion template: "De Luca Lower Third"

**Outro card (2 seconds):**
- Background: #6E0B1C
- Centre: silver logo badge "De Luca" 
- Optionally: "delucacucina.co.uk" in DM Sans Light below
- Save as a compound clip: "De Luca Outro"

**Colour grade (save as preset):**
- Lift blacks slightly (add warmth to shadows)
- Warm mids (+8–12 temperature)
- Slightly crush highlights
- Name: "De Luca Warm"

### Workflow:
1. Import footage
2. Rough cut in timeline
3. Apply "De Luca Warm" colour preset
4. Add Lower Third where needed
5. Drop in De Luca Outro at end
6. Export: File → Share → Master File → then Compressor or Handbrake for Instagram-optimised MP4
