# De Luca — Adobe Express Template Specs

## Brand Kit Setup (do this first, once)

**Colours to save:**
- Primary: #6E0B1C (Crimson)
- Secondary: #C0C0C0 (Silver)
- White: #FFFFFF
- Near Black: #1A1A1A

**Fonts:**
- Display/captions: Cormorant Garamond — Italic
- UI/labels: DM Sans — Light (300 weight)

**Logo element:**
- Rectangle: fill #C0C0C0, no border radius, horizontal (roughly 3:1 ratio)
- Text on top: "De Luca" in DM Sans Light, #6E0B1C, letter-spacing 0.05em
- Save this as a grouped element in your Adobe Express brand kit

---

## TEMPLATE 1: Dark Solid Story
**File name:** DL-Story-DarkSolid
**Dimensions:** 1080 × 1920px (Instagram Story)

### Setup:
1. New design → Custom size → 1080 × 1920
2. Background: solid colour → #6E0B1C
3. Text element (caption):
   - Font: Cormorant Garamond Italic
   - Colour: #FFFFFF
   - Size: 72–84px
   - Position: upper-left, ~100px from left/top
   - Line 1: e.g. "The view?"
   - Line 2 (indented ~80px): e.g. "Bellissimo."
4. Logo badge (from brand kit): bottom-centre, ~100px from bottom
5. Save as template

### Weekly use:
- Open template, change caption text only
- Export as JPG 1080×1920
- Post as Story Monday 9–11am

## TEMPLATE 2: White Border Story
**File name:** DL-Story-WhiteBorder
**Dimensions:** 1080 × 1920px

### Setup:
1. Background: #FFFFFF
2. Rectangle: no fill, stroke colour #6E0B1C, stroke width 20px
   - Position: 40px margin all sides → size 1000 × 1840
3. Caption text:
   - Font: Cormorant Garamond Italic
   - Colour: #6E0B1C
   - Size: 64–72px
   - Position: inside border, upper area (~180px from top)
4. Optional photo area: centre of canvas, inside border
5. Logo badge: bottom-centre inside border, ~100px from bottom of canvas
6. Save as template

### Weekly use:
- Open template, change caption text
- Optionally add a photo in the centre
- Export as JPG or PNG

## TEMPLATE 3: Photo + Caption Story
**File name:** DL-Story-PhotoCaption
**Dimensions:** 1080 × 1920px

### Setup:
1. Background: #FFFFFF
2. Image element:
   - Top of canvas
   - Width: 1080px, Height: ~1150px (leaves ~770px below for text)
   - Placeholder/lock to keep proportions
3. White area below image (natural from background):
   - No element needed — background is white
4. Caption text:
   - Position: 80px below image bottom edge
   - Font: Cormorant Garamond Italic
   - Colour: #6E0B1C
   - Size: 52–60px
   - Width: ~900px, centred
5. Logo badge: bottom-centre, 80px from canvas bottom
6. Save as template

### Weekly use:
- Click photo placeholder → replace with your photo
- Update caption
- Export

## TEMPLATE 4: Circular Food Feed Post
**File name:** DL-Feed-CircularFood
**Dimensions:** 1080 × 1080px (Instagram Square)

### Setup:
1. Background: #6E0B1C
2. Image element:
   - Click image → Crop → Circle/round crop
   - Size: ~620px diameter
   - Position: centre-left or upper-centre
3. Caption text:
   - Font: Cormorant Garamond Italic
   - Colour: #FFFFFF
   - Size: 60–72px
   - Position: lower-right or below circle, depending on image
4. Logo badge: bottom-centre or bottom-right, 60px from edge
5. Save as template

### Weekly use:
- Replace circle image
- Update caption
- Export as JPG 1080×1080

## TEMPLATE 5: Split Layout Feed Post
**File name:** DL-Feed-SplitLayout
**Dimensions:** 1080 × 1350px (4:5 ratio — best for feed reach)

### Setup:
1. Background: #FFFFFF
2. Left panel rectangle:
   - Fill: #6E0B1C
   - Size: 540 × 1350px
   - Position: left edge, full height
3. Right panel: add image element:
   - Size: 540 × 1350px
   - Position: right half
4. Caption text (on crimson panel):
   - Font: Cormorant Garamond Italic
   - Colour: #FFFFFF
   - Size: 52–60px
   - Position: lower-left area of crimson panel
5. Logo badge: lower-left of crimson panel
6. Save as template

## TEMPLATE 6: Full Photo Overlay Feed Post
**File name:** DL-Feed-FullPhotoOverlay
**Dimensions:** 1080 × 1350px

### Setup:
1. Full bleed image: 1080 × 1350px, fills entire canvas
2. Gradient overlay rectangle:
   - Size: 1080 × 600px (bottom 44%)
   - Fill: gradient — top transparent, bottom #6E0B1C at 85% opacity
   - Position: bottom of canvas
3. Caption text:
   - Font: Cormorant Garamond Italic
   - Colour: #FFFFFF
   - Size: 56px
   - Position: on gradient area, centred
4. Logo badge: above caption, or bottom-centre after caption
5. Save as template
