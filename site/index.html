Fri/Sat nights, cocktails, candlelight, Robin P-iano, sing-alongs. Moody atmospheric shots.

CapCut / Final Cut — Reel formats

🍝 Dish Reveal Reel · 8–12s

Structure: Black frame → slow zoom onto dish → Italian serif title card (crimson bg) → De Luca logo fade out.
Audio: Trending Italian/ambient food audio.
Caption: Short. "Handmade. Every time." + hashtags.

🌇 Terrace Timelapse · 15–20s

Structure: Wide terrace shot day → dusk → evening lights → text overlay "Dinner with a view" → booking CTA slide.
Audio: Soft ambient/lo-fi.
Caption: "The Cambridge rooftop you didn't know you needed."

🎹 Piano Bar Night · 15–30s

Structure: Cocktail glass → piano keys → crowd shots → singer → "Every Friday & Saturday" title card.
Audio: Clip of live performance or upbeat jazz.
Caption: "Cambridge's favourite Friday night just got better."

👨‍🍳 Behind the Scenes · 20–30s

Structure: Raw ingredients → chef hands → pasta being made → plated dish → open kitchen energy.
Audio: Kitchen sounds + upbeat track.
Caption: "Made from scratch. Every single day."

Voice & tone

✓ DO

  • Italian-inflected words (Bellissimo, Salute, Cucina)
  • Rhetorical questions ("Fancy a bite?")
  • Short punchy lines — let the photo lead
  • Warm and direct, like a charming host
  • Reference Cambridge ("rooftop views of Cambridge")
  • Celebrate occasions (graduation, date night)

✕ DON'T

  • Discount language ("CHEAP", "DEAL", "SALE")
  • Emoji overload
  • Generic hashtags (#foodie #yummy #instagood)
  • Corporate/formal tone
  • Long captions (keep under 200 chars for feed)
  • Caps lock shouting

Scheduling workflow
1

Sunday — Batch design (20 min)

Open Adobe Express. Use saved templates. Drop in this week's photo. Adjust caption from the caption bank. Export all 3 pieces (2 stories + 1 feed post) to your phone.

2

Sunday — Schedule in Meta Business Suite (10 min)

Go to business.facebook.com → Posts → Schedule. Story 1: Monday 9–11am. Story 2: Wednesday 9–11am. Feed post: Friday 5–7pm. Done for the week.

3

If posting a Reel — Thursday evening

Edit in CapCut (quick) or Final Cut (polished). Export 1080×1920 MP4. Post manually Friday or use Buffer/Later free tier to schedule it.

4

Monday + Friday — Engagement (5 min each)

Reply to all comments and DMs. Like comments. Follow back relevant local accounts. This takes 5 minutes and dramatically boosts reach.

Toolkit
Ae

Adobe Express — daily driver

Your main design tool. Build the 6 templates once, reuse forever. Save brand colours, fonts and logo in the brand kit.

  • Set brand kit: #6E0B1C crimson + silver + Cormorant Garamond
  • Upload De Luca logo as a saved element
  • Build all 6 templates, save as "De Luca Story 1" etc.
  • Animate text on stories using the Motion tool
  • Export: 1080×1920 (story) · 1080×1080 (feed)
CC

CapCut — quick reels

Fast mobile video editing. Create a De Luca "style pack" with saved text presets and transitions.

  • Save crimson text style as preset (Cormorant Garamond, italic)
  • Save DM Sans lower-thirds style
  • Use "Auto Captions" for behind-the-scenes reels
  • Keep transitions: cut or simple fade only (no flashy)
  • Export: 1080×1920, 30fps, H.264
FC

Final Cut Pro — polished reels

For Piano Bar highlight films and seasonal campaigns. Set up a De Luca project with colour grade and branded lower thirds.

  • Create project: 1080×1920, 30fps
  • Add crimson colour grade (lift blacks, warm mids)
  • Build lower thirds title template in Motion
  • Keep a "De Luca Outro" clip (logo fade out, 2s)
  • Export: Master File → then compress for web
MB

Meta Business Suite — scheduling

Free. Manages Instagram + Facebook together. Schedule everything for the week in one 10-minute Sunday session.

  • Go to business.facebook.com (or mobile app)
  • Planner view → Create post → Schedule
  • Stories can be scheduled (Instagram only)
  • Inbox tab handles DMs from both platforms
  • Insights tab → check reach every Monday
